CHOOSING YOUR TRAVEL DESTINATIONS

When choosing your travel destinations, it depends on factors like:

  1. your personal travel style
  2. your interests
  3. the length of time you have available
  4. your finances.

Socially Responsible Travel

At Global Exchange Reality Tours, we talk a lot about ‘socially responsible’ travel. Before leaving home, learn as much as possible about the country you are visiting and respect the local environment.
